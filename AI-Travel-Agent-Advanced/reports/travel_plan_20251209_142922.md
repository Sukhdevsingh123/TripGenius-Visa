    *   **14:30:** Explore **Dwarkadhish Temple**, one of Mathura's oldest and most prominent temples, known for its vibrant architecture and festive atmosphere.
    *   **16:00:** Head to **Vishram Ghat** on the Yamuna River. This is where Lord Krishna is believed to have rested after slaying Kansa. Enjoy the serene atmosphere, watch locals, and consider a boat ride (optional).
*   **Evening (18:00 - 21:00): Yamuna Aarti & Local Flavors**
    *   **18:30:** Witness the mesmerizing **Yamuna Aarti at Vishram Ghat**. Arrive early to secure a good spot for this traditional and intimate ceremony.

    *   **21:00:** Return to Hotel Brijwasi Royal for the night.

*   **Morning (07:00 - 12:00): Transfer to Vrindavan & Early Temple Visits**
    *   **07:00:** Check out from Mathura hotel. Take an e-rickshaw or auto-rickshaw to Vrindavan (approx. 30-45 minutes).
    *   **08:00:** Check into Hotel Nidhivan Sarovar Portico (or similar) in Vrindavan. Drop luggage.
    *   **09:00:** Visit **Banke Bihari Temple**. Aim for an early visit to experience the morning darshan (viewing of the deity) with fewer crowds. This temple is famous for its unique 'pardah' system where the deity is revealed and hidden frequently.
    *   **10:30:** Explore **Seva Kunj and Nidhuban**. These interconnected gardens are steeped in legend, believed to be the site of Krishna and Radha's nightly Ras Leela. Experience the quiet contemplation and unique Tulsi plants.
    *   **Breakfast (08:00 - 09:00, or after Banke Bihari):**
        *   **Recommendation:** Enjoy a simple breakfast at your hotel or find a local eatery for **Puri-Sabzi** or **Lassi**.
        *   **Estimated Cost:** INR 200-300.
*   **Afternoon (12:00 - 18:00): Hidden Gems & Spiritual Serenity**
    *   **12:00:** Visit **Gopishwar Mahadev Temple**. This ancient Shiva temple holds a unique place in Vrindavan's lore, where Lord Shiva is said to have taken the form of a Gopi.
    *   **13:30:** Lunch
        *   **Recommendation:** **Govinda's Restaurant** at ISKCON Temple for a sattvic and delicious vegetarian meal.
        *   **Estimated Cost:** INR 400-600.
    *   **15:00:** Relax at the hotel during the afternoon temple closing hours (12 PM to 4 PM) or explore **Loi Bazaar** for souvenirs and religious items.
    *   **16:30:** Visit **ISKCON Vrindavan (Sri Krishna Balaram Mandir)**. Experience the beautiful architecture, serene atmosphere, and the chanting of Hare Krishna devotees.
*   **Evening (18:00 - 21:00): Sunset & Devotion**
    *   **18:00:** Head to **Kesi Ghat** for a serene sunset experience. Witness locals performing rituals and offering lamps to the Yamuna River, a magical atmosphere away from the main temple crowds.
    *   **19:30:** Attend the evening aarti at **Prem Mandir**. While not a traditional aarti, the temple's magnificent architecture and colorful light show are breathtaking.
    *   **Dinner (20:30 - 21:30):**
        *   **Recommendation:** Explore local restaurants near Prem Mandir for traditional North Indian vegetarian dishes or try **Rabri and Malpua** from a local sweet shop.
        *   **Estimated Cost:** INR 500-700.
    *   **21:30:** Return to Hotel Nidhivan Sarovar Portico.

### **Practical Information**

*   **Weather-appropriate packing suggestions:** January in Mathura Vrindavan can be quite chilly, especially in the mornings and evenings. Pack warm clothes: sweaters, jackets, shawls, and thermals. Comfortable slip-on shoes are advisable as you'll be removing them frequently at temples.
